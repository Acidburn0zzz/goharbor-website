Distribution lists

There are two distribution lists for Harbor, one for users and one for developers:

User groups

Join Harbor's user email group at https://lists.cncf.io/g/harbor-users to get Harbor's news, features, and releases, or to provide suggestions and feedback.

Developer groups

Join Harbor's developer group at https://lists.cncf.io/g/harbor-dev for updates related to development and contribution.
